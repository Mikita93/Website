Top Sights to See in Minsk

Theaters, museums, excursions, and more await in this welcoming

History

From Victory Square to Independence Square travel down one of the longest streets in Europe, Independence Avenue, and turn right to the historical district of Nemiga. Explore memorials, statues, and soak in the history of the country’s past. Take a trip to the famous Great Patriotic War Museum, and then grab a coffee and admire the sunset.

Culture

Take in a show at the National Opera and Ballet Theatre, head to Minsk arena, explore the Art Museum, or climb to the top of the National Library, shaped like a spaceship. Discover a whole new world and enjoy all Minsk has to offer.

Cosmopolitan

Shopping, spas, entertainment and more await any eager traveller to this hidden gem. Minsk is packed with shopping centers, cinemas, and spas giving everyone and anyone the opportunity to find an activity to suit their needs and mood. From the biggest VR park in Europe to vertical wind tunnel tube, and so much more. You’ll be surprised at what Minsk has to offer.
