ons to Visit Minsk

You’ll be surprised at what you’ll find in the city that’s home to 2 million people

Soviet, but so modern

Soak in the Soviet architecture and history with the comfort of top-class service and attractions. Eat, drink, and explore in an exciting, safe city.

Festivals, Festivals, Festivals

Love coffee? We’ve got a fest for that. Vegan food? Right this way. Old-timey recreations? We got you. Hackathon? Jump right in. Minsk has something for everyone.

Museums, theatres, concerts

Natural history, the Great Patriotic War, Doll Theatre, and the Great Opera House, find all this and so much more in Minsk.
