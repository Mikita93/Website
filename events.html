Events in Minsk

Minsk hosts a variety of events throughout the year, ranging from cultural and sports events to music festivals and exhibitions. Let’s take a look at some of the most interesting events in Minsk.


Minsk International Book Fair - Usually held in February, this event is a major gathering of publishers, authors, and book enthusiasts from Belarus and other countries.


International Women's Day - Celebrated on March 8, this day is a public holiday in Belarus and is marked with various events and activities.


Minsk Half Marathon - This popular sports event takes place in April, attracting runners from all over the world.


Coffee Fest - This is an annual festival where various coffee shop throughout the city create unique desserts and coffee to sell at the festival.


Minsk International Film Festival "Listapad" - Usually held in November, this event is a major gathering of filmmakers and film enthusiasts from Belarus and other countries.
