Interesting facts about Minsk

Minsk is the capital and largest city of Belarus, a country located in Eastern Europe. It is a vibrant city, full of theaters, parks, entertainment, and more. The top 5 facts about Minsk? Let us tell you!

Minsk is the largest city in Belarus, with a population of over 2 million people.

The city was founded in 1067, making it over 950 years old.

Minsk was almost completely destroyed during World War II, and was subsequently rebuilt in the Stalinist architectural style.

The city has a rich cultural heritage, with numerous museums, theaters, and art galleries.
